What is GPA?

GPA is a number that shows how well you are doing in your studies. The higher your GPA, the better your grades are.

This website helps you calculate your GPA quickly and easily, so you always know how well you did.

How Does GPA Calculation Work?

Each course you take has a number of hours. Your grade in each course is changed to grade points based on the GPA scale.

To calculate your GPA, multiply the grade points of each course by its hours. Then divide the total by the number of hours.
